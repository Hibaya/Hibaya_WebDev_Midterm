The Lack Of Happiness

Sometimes people might think that you are a failure
Because of your personality that they judge
People might think that you cannot do whatever they can do
Because you mess up always that will not be corrected


People might think that you are crazy
Because no matter what you say or do it will always fail
Many people discourages you to do what you like
Because If you do they will be afraid that you can do it right


People are saying you are being use
Because many people sees me only for the money that I get and for giving them
Many people know what its like to be alone
But people will never know true loneliness when they cannot experience what its like to be shut by the world.
